Description

A short story written by Nick Joaquin, a renowned Filipino writer known for his insightful explorations of Philippine society and culture. The story revolves around the character D.M. Guevara, a successful businessman who is revered and feared in his community. D.M. Guevara is portrayed as a complex figure, wielding power and influence with a combination of charm and ruthlessness. Through his rise to prominence, the story delves into themes of ambition, corruption, and the pursuit of wealth in Philippine society.

Summary

As the narrative unfolds, readers are drawn into Guevara's world, where he navigates the intricacies of business and politics with cunning and determination. However, beneath his facade of success lies a darker reality, as Guevara grapples with the consequences of his actions and the sacrifices he has made along the way. Through Guevara's story, Joaquin offers a nuanced portrayal of the Filipino elite and the complexities of power dynamics in Philippine society. The story serves as a commentary on the pursuit of success at any cost, as well as the moral dilemmas faced by individuals in positions of authority.
