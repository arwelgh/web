Description

A biographical work that celebrates the life and legacy of Nicanor Reyes, the visionary founder of Far Eastern University (F.E.U.) in the Philippines. Joaquin's narrative dives into Reyes's remarkable journey from humble beginnings to becoming a pioneering figure in Philippine education. The book illuminates Reyes's deep commitment to providing quality education accessible to all, regardless of social or economic status. It explores his innovative approach to education, which emphasized practical skills alongside academic excellence, anticipating the needs of a rapidly changing society.

Summary

A vivid portrait of Reyes as a cultural icon, not only for his contributions to education but also for his broader impact on Philippine society. Through his leadership, Reyes fostered a vibrant intellectual and artistic community at F.E.U., nurturing talent and creativity across various fields. The book celebrates Reyes's enduring legacy as a trailblazer in Philippine education and culture, whose vision continues to inspire generations of students and educators.
