Description

A captivating exploration of the vibrant and complex history of Manila, tailored specifically for young readers. Through engaging narrative and colorful illustrations, this book takes its audience on a journey through the evolution of Manila, from its early days as a bustling trading port to its status as a modern metropolis. Readers will discover the rich tapestry of cultures that have shaped Manila over the centuries, from indigenous Filipino communities to Spanish colonial influences and beyond. They will learn about key historical events, such as the arrival of the Spanish conquistadors, the American occupation, and the struggles for independence.

Summary

It imagines Manila as the continuously endangered seed of the nation, which miraculously overcomes the multitude of threats thrown its way. While the narrative glosses over the inherent diversity of the nation, it also exposes an essentialist, teleological, and metaphysical historical vision.
