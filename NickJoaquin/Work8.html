Description

A thought-provoking tale that offers insight into the human condition and the complexities of belief. Joaquin's masterful storytelling and rich characterizations make it a compelling read that lingers in the mind long after the final page. Through Cándido's journey, Joaquin explores themes of faith, delusion, and the search for meaning in a world fraught with uncertainty.

Summary

The story is set in a Philippine town, where Cándido, a simple and devout man, becomes convinced that he is destined to bring about the end of the world. He gathers a small group of followers who believe in his prophecy, and they embark on a journey to fulfill his mission. As they travel through the countryside, Cándido and his followers encounter various challenges and obstacles, including skepticism from others and doubts within their own ranks. Despite these challenges, Cándido remains steadfast in his belief and continues to preach his message of impending doom.
